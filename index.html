About Signify

Signify is an innovative solution designed to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf or hard-of-hearing community. By translating Indian Sign Language (ISL) into text and speech in real-time, Signify empowers users to communicate effortlessly across language barriers.

Key USPs:

  • Real-Time Translation: Fast and accurate conversion of ISL gestures to text and speech.
  • Multilingual Support: Supports output in multiple Indian languages.
  • Scalable & Flexible: Designed for use in various scenarios, including online education, social interaction, and professional environments.
  • Educational Integration: Seamless integration with online learning platforms to enhance accessibility for disabled students.

Viability:

Signify is a versatile and scalable platform that can be deployed across various sectors, from education to law enforcement. Its robust technology ensures high accuracy and adaptability, making it an essential tool for improving accessibility and inclusivity in communication.
